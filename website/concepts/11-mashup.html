📌 Pinned
First window always goes to a fixed workspace. Open another window? That one lands wherever you are.
e.g. Teams → always Workspace 2
🧲 Follow
Focus the window and it moves to your current workspace. It travels with you, wherever you go.
e.g. Finder — wherever you are, it comes to you
📍 Stay
Windows stay on the workspace where you placed them. Switch away, they hide. Switch back, they appear.
e.g. Safari, VS Code — right where you left them
Hands on keyboard.
Eyes on work.
